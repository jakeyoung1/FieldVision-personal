The Pipeline

How It Works

Three AI-powered steps from handwritten note to structured scouting intelligence

Step 01

OCR Transcription

Upload a photo or scan of handwritten scouting notes. Claude Vision reads every abbreviation, player name, and shorthand — even messy field notes — with high accuracy.

Step 02

Semantic Retrieval

Query is matched against 1,919 Branch Rickey scouting documents using TF-IDF cosine similarity (20K-feature sklearn index). Top-5 most relevant reports retrieved.

Step 03

AI Report Generation

Claude Sonnet 4.5 synthesizes your notes with the retrieved historical context to generate a summary, two actionable recommendations, and a Branch Rickey parallel insight.

Capabilities

Built for Coaches

Every tool a coaching staff needs to turn raw scouting into decisions

Handwritten Note Upload

Upload PDF, TXT, or MD scouting notes. Multi-page PDF support included. Vision OCR handles messy handwriting, abbreviations, and field shorthand.

Branch Rickey RAG

1,919 historical scouting transcriptions from the Branch Rickey Papers indexed with TF-IDF. Every report is benchmarked against decades of professional scouting wisdom.

Trackman Game Analysis

Upload Trackman CSV exports for post-game pitch analytics. Velocity, spin rate, break, and pitch mix summaries for both teams' pitchers — now live and approved.

AI Chat Interface

Ask follow-up questions about any uploaded material. Claude stays grounded in your session context — your notes, Trackman data, and retrieved history all inform responses.

PDF Report Export

Download a formatted PDF of any generated scouting report. Clean layout with section headers, recommendations, and historical parallels — ready to share.

Session Memory

Upload multiple notes across a session — context accumulates. The AI sees all prior uploads as background when answering questions, preventing repeated explanations.
