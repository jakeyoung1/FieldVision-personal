Cole Braddock
RHP, junior, 6'3 205. Fastball sits 94-96, touched 97 twice this spring — ride through the zone, misses bats up. Slider is the out pitch, mid-80s with late two-plane bite; lands it for strikes and buries it with two strikes. Changeup flashes average, arm speed holds but it stays firm. Delivery is clean and repeatable, slight crossfire. Competes like a bulldog — wanted the ball in the ninth against Pepperdine. Command grades ahead of control; will nibble when he doesn't trust the changeup. Frame projects five more pounds. Draft follow.

Danny Vasquez
RHP, sophomore, 6'1 190. Sinkerballer, 90-93 with heavy run and sink — gets ground balls in bunches, 62% GB rate this year. Curveball is a true 12-6 hammer at 76-79, freezes hitters. Cutter is new this year, keeps lefties honest. Doesn't miss many bats but doesn't need to; three-pitch mix plays fast in the zone. Fielding is plus, controls the run game well. Gets in trouble when the sinker flattens up in the zone. Durable — went 7+ three times in conference play. Weekend starter profile at the next level.
